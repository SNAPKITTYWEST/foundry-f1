## Commercial Meaning

They are buying:

- commercial deployment rights
- access to closed proof work product
- private verification artifacts
- WORM-grade provenance and attestation packaging
- support, patching, and controlled distribution rights

## Packaging Language

Use this phrasing in commercial contexts:

`Exclusive proof-backed governance package with 13 formerly open ALP obligations packaged as licensable certainty assets.`

Avoid weaker framing like "open research bundle" if this is being sold
as a commercial rights package.
